Watermelon Cooler Slushy

This delicous slushy that you can drink on a summer day!

  • Ingrediants

  • 4 cups cube seedless watermelon
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Place watermelon and ice into a blender. Pour in lime juice, sugar, and salt. Blend until smooth.
